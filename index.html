Gameplay Overview

You basically take random sounds that aren't music and turn them into something that aren't music and turn them into something that actually sounds good. Sounds are collected and unlocked through levels. Each level introduces a new sound set

Interaction & Controls

  • Tap to add or remove a sound

  • Sliders or knobs for volume and rhythm

  • It's pretty easy to figure out just drag things around and see what happens

  • You hear changes instantly as you tweak stuff
